// WHO I AM

About

I'm Hans Ade — a software engineer, AI builder, and founder based in Cameroon. I'm the Founder & CTO of PiPilot, an AI fullstack build hub that turns plain-language prompts into deployed web apps, mobile apps, desktop apps, and APIs. I also co-founded Pixelways Inc, a digital solutions & IT consulting company.

Through Pixelways, I've built and shipped products for clients across Cameroon, Canada, and beyond — from e-commerce platforms and nonprofit sites to construction portfolios and multi-marketplace apps. I care about clean architecture, real-world performance, and software that actually ships.
