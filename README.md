# Hans Ade — Founder & CTO

## About Me

I'm Hans Ade — a software engineer, AI builder, and founder based in Cameroon. I'm the **Founder & CTO of [PiPilot](https://pipilot.dev)**, an AI fullstack build hub that turns plain-language prompts into deployed web apps, mobile apps, desktop apps, and APIs. I also co-founded **[Pixelways Inc](https://pixelways.co)**, a digital solutions & IT consulting company.

Through Pixelways, I've built and shipped products for clients across Cameroon, Canada, and beyond — from e-commerce platforms and nonprofit sites to construction portfolios and multi-marketplace apps. I care about clean architecture, real-world performance, and software that actually ships.

## Products

### [PiPilot](https://pipilot.dev) — Founder & CTO

AI fullstack build hub. Describe an idea in chat and the agent builds, deploys, and scales it — web apps, React Native / Expo mobile apps (Android APK and iOS via EAS), Tauri and Electron desktop installers, Express and FastAPI backends, CLIs, and npm packages. Not a mockup, not a sandbox toy, but software you can ship. Backed by E2B for Startups.

### [Pixelways Inc](https://pixelways.co) — Co-Founder

Digital solutions & IT consulting — custom software development, cloud & DevOps, AI & data analytics, UI/UX design, e-commerce, security & compliance, and technical advisory for businesses across Africa and North America.
